Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

At Farm Vantara, we collect information to provide better services to all our users - farmers, businesses, and consumers. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide: When you register for an account, we ask for personal information like your name, email address, telephone number, and address.
  • Transaction information: When you buy or sell through Farm Vantara, we collect information about the transaction.
  • Information we get from your use of our services: We collect information about the services that you use and how you use them, like when you visit our website or view and interact with our content.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from all our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect Farm Vantara and our users. We also use this information to offer you tailored content.

  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To process your transactions
  • To provide customer support
  • To send you important notices, such as communications about purchases and changes to our terms, conditions, and policies
  • For internal purposes such as auditing, data analysis, and research to improve our products, services, and customer communications

3. Information Security

We work hard to protect Farm Vantara and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt many of our services using SSL
  • We review our information collection, storage and processing practices, including physical security measures, to guard against unauthorized access to systems
  • We restrict access to personal information to Farm Vantara employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations

4. Changes to Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes).

Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

1. Quality Guarantee

Farm Vantara guarantees the quality of all products sold on our platform. If you're not satisfied with the quality of produce received, you're eligible for:

  • Full refund within 24 hours of delivery
  • Partial refund for minor quality issues
  • Replacement delivery at no extra cost

2. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the quality issue
  3. Our quality team will review within 2 hours
  4. If approved, pickup will be scheduled within 24 hours
  5. Refund processed within 3-5 business days

3. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable items after 24 hours of delivery
  • Customized or personalized orders
  • Damaged due to customer mishandling

4. Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI: 24-48 hours
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • Wallet: Instant to 24 hours

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I register as a farmer on Farm Vantara?

To register as a farmer, visit our Farmers page and click "Register Free". You'll need to provide basic information, farm details, identity proof, and bank details for payments. Our team will verify your details within 24 hours and activate your account.

What are the commission charges for farmers?

Farm Vantara charges a nominal 2% commission on successful transactions. There are no registration fees, monthly fees, or hidden charges. First 10 transactions are completely free for new farmers.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location: Metro cities (24-48 hours), Tier 2 cities (48-72 hours), other areas (3-5 business days). Same-day delivery is available in select cities for orders placed before 12 PM.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods: Credit/Debit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and Digital Wallets. Payments to farmers are released within 24 hours of successful delivery.

How do I report a quality issue?

Report quality issues within 24 hours of delivery through your order history or contact customer support at 1800-123-4567. Please provide photos of the issue for faster resolution.
