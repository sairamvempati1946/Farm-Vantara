Precision Agriculture

AI-Powered Crop Disease Detection Using Drone Imagery

Dr. Amit Patel, Dr. Neha Gupta, et al.

Development of a convolutional neural network that analyzes drone-captured multispectral imagery to detect crop diseases with 94% accuracy, 3 weeks before visible symptoms appear to farmers.

Impact: Early detection reduces pesticide use by 40% and crop losses by 60%.

Water Conservation

Optimizing Drip Irrigation Schedules Using IoT and Machine Learning

Dr. Sanjay Mehta, Dr. Anjali Reddy, et al.

A real-time irrigation optimization system that integrates soil moisture sensors, weather data, and crop water requirements to reduce water usage by 45% while maintaining or improving crop yields across different agro-climatic zones.

Savings: Average water savings of 1.2 million liters per hectare annually.
