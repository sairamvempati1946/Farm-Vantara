Our 3-Tier Quality Process

Comprehensive verification at every stage of the supply chain

Pre-Harvest Verification

Quality begins at the farm. Our field officers verify farming practices, soil conditions, and crop health before harvest.

  • Soil testing & analysis
  • Water quality verification
  • Farming practice audit
  • Pesticide & fertilizer check
  • Crop health assessment
  • Harvest timing verification

Harvest & Post-Harvest Inspection

Rigorous inspection during and after harvest to ensure optimal quality and proper handling.

  • Harvest quality assessment
  • Grading & sorting verification
  • Packaging standards check
  • Storage condition audit
  • Temperature control monitoring
  • Handling practice verification

Delivery & Final Verification

Final quality check before delivery and verification upon receipt to ensure customer satisfaction.

  • Pre-shipment quality check
  • Transport condition monitoring
  • Cold chain verification
  • Final quality certification
  • Customer acceptance verification
  • Feedback collection & analysis
