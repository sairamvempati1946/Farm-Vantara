Available Payment Methods

Choose the payment method that best suits your transaction needs. All methods are secure and transparent.

UPI Payments

Instant & Most Popular

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) allows instant bank-to-bank transfers using mobile apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM. Most recommended for transactions under ₹2 lakhs.

Transaction Speed Instant
Transaction Limit ₹1-2 lakhs/day
Transaction Fees Zero charges
Best For Small to medium transactions
RECOMMENDED: Fastest & most convenient for daily transactions

Escrow Protection Service

Farm Vantara Exclusive

Our secure escrow service holds payment until produce delivery is confirmed. Once buyer verifies quality and quantity, payment is released to farmer. Eliminates payment risks for both parties.

Payment Release After delivery verification
Transaction Limit No limit
Service Fee 1% (capped at ₹5,000)
Dispute Resolution Included
HIGHLY RECOMMENDED: Best for new relationships & large transactions

Bank Transfer

Traditional & Reliable

Direct bank transfers via NEFT, RTGS, or IMPS. NEFT operates in batches, RTGS for large amounts (₹2 lakhs+), and IMPS for instant transfers. Bank statements serve as transaction proof.

Transaction Speed 2hrs - Instant
Transaction Limit No limit (RTGS)
Bank Charges ₹2-25 + GST
Best For Large transactions
RECOMMENDED: Ideal for transactions over ₹2 lakhs

Digital Wallets

Mobile Convenience

Paytm, PhonePe Wallet, Amazon Pay, and other mobile wallets. Money is stored in the wallet and can be transferred instantly. KYC verification required for larger transactions.

Transaction Speed Instant
Wallet Limit ₹1-2 lakhs (with KYC)
Transaction Fees Zero for wallet to wallet
Best For Quick small payments
GOOD ALTERNATIVE: Convenient but has wallet balance limits

QR Code Payments

Contactless & Easy

Static or dynamic QR codes that can be scanned with any UPI app. Farmers can display printed QR codes at collection points. Buyers scan and pay instantly without sharing details.

Transaction Speed Instant
Transaction Limit Same as UPI limits
Setup Required QR code generation
Best For In-person transactions
RECOMMENDED: Excellent for collection centers & mandis

Cheque/Demand Draft

Traditional Paper

Physical cheques or demand drafts. Cheques take 2-3 days to clear, demand drafts are pre-paid instruments. Provides physical proof but has clearing time and bounce risks.

Clearing Time 2-3 business days
Risk Factor Cheque bounce risk
Bank Charges ₹50-200 per instrument
Best For Traditional businesses
LIMITED USE: Only when digital options not available

Payment FAQs

Common questions about payments on Farm Vantara

How quickly do farmers receive payments?

Payment timing depends on the method used: UPI & Digital Wallets are instant; Escrow Service releases within 24 hours of delivery verification; Bank transfers take 2 hours to 1 business day; Cheques require 2-3 business days for clearing. We guarantee payment to farmers within 24 hours when using Farm Vantara's payment services.

Are there any transaction fees charged by Farm Vantara?

Farm Vantara does not charge any fees for UPI, bank transfers, digital wallets, or QR code payments. We only charge a 1% service fee (capped at ₹5,000) for our Escrow Protection Service, which includes payment security, dispute resolution, and guaranteed payment release. Bank charges for RTGS/NEFT/IMPS are levied by banks, not by Farm Vantara.

What if a payment fails or gets delayed?

In case of payment failure: 1) Check transaction status in your payment app/bank, 2) Contact our 24/7 payment support at 1800-123-4567, 3) For Escrow Service, we mediate between parties, 4) Failed transactions are automatically reversed within 30 minutes. We provide transaction tracking and immediate notification for any delays. For disputes, our resolution team intervenes within 2 hours.

How do I set up UPI or QR code for my farm?

Setting up UPI/QR is simple: 1) Download any UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, BHIM), 2) Link your bank account, 3) Create UPI ID (usually mobile@bankname), 4) In Farm Vantara profile, add your UPI ID, 5) We generate a unique QR code for you. For farmers without smartphones, we provide printed QR codes that can be scanned by buyers. Our support team assists with setup in regional languages.

What payment records will I receive?

For every transaction, you receive: 1) Farm Vantara transaction receipt with details, 2) Payment confirmation from your bank/UPI app, 3) Monthly payment statements for accounting, 4) Annual transaction summary for tax purposes. All records are available in your account dashboard and can be downloaded as PDF. Farmers also receive SMS confirmation for every payment received.
